Why Parasite Control is Important?

Parasites like fleas, ticks, and worms can cause serious health issues in pets including itching, infections, anemia, and digestive problems. Regular prevention keeps your pet safe and healthy.

Common Parasites

Fleas
Ticks
Intestinal Worms
Mites
Heartworms

Warning Signs

Excessive scratching, hair loss, weight loss, vomiting, diarrhea, or visible insects on skin may indicate parasite infection.

Prevention Methods

Regular deworming, flea & tick treatments, clean environment, and routine vet checkups help prevent parasite infections.
