Healthy Food = Healthy Pets 🐶🐱

Why Pet Nutrition Matters?

Proper nutrition is essential for your pet’s growth, immunity, energy, and overall health. A balanced diet helps prevent diseases and increases lifespan.

Basic Pet Diet Guidelines

High-quality protein
Balanced carbohydrates
Healthy fats
Vitamins & minerals
Fresh water daily

Common Vet-Suggested Supplements (Consult Vet First)

Multivitamins
Omega-3 (Skin & Coat)
Probiotics (Digestion)
Calcium Supplements
Joint Support (Glucosamine)

⚠️ Always consult a veterinarian before giving any supplement or medicine.

Foods to Avoid ❌

Chocolate, onions, garlic, grapes, alcohol, caffeine, and processed junk food can be harmful or toxic to pets.
