⚠️ This guide is NOT a substitute for veterinary care. In serious cases, contact a vet immediately.

🚨 Emergency Warning Signs

Difficulty Breathing
Excessive Bleeding
Seizures
Sudden Collapse
Poison Ingestion
Continuous Vomiting

🔍 Basic At-Home Checks (Non-Diagnostic)

These checks help you observe your pet’s condition before visiting a vet.

Check breathing rate
Check gum color
Check body temperature (touch)
Look for wounds or swelling
Observe behavior changes

🩹 Basic First Aid Tips

Keep your pet calm, avoid giving human medicines, stop bleeding with clean cloth, and move the pet safely to a vet clinic.

Need Immediate Help?

Contact a veterinarian right away.
