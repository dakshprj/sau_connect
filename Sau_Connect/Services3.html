About Air Pollution and Birds

Birds are highly sensitive to air quality. Pollutants like smog and toxins disrupt migration, breeding, and survival. Protecting birds means fighting pollution for a healthier planet.

Impacts of Air Pollution on Birds

Respiratory Damage

Pollutants cause lung issues, reducing bird lifespan and flight ability.

Habitat Disruption

Acid rain and toxins destroy nesting sites and food sources.

Migration Interference

Climate change from pollution alters migration patterns and routes.

How You Can Help

  • Reduce Emissions: Use public transport, bike, or walk to cut vehicle pollution.
  • Plant Trees: Support reforestation to improve air quality and bird habitats.
  • Advocate: Support clean air policies and organizations like the Audubon Society.
  • Educate: Share facts about pollution's effects on birds.
