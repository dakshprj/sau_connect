About Aquatic Conservation

Aquatic animals are vital to our ecosystem, but they're endangered by human impact. From plastic pollution to habitat destruction, we must act now to preserve marine biodiversity.

Major Threats to Aquatic Animals

Plastic Pollution

Millions of tons of plastic harm marine life, from turtles to fish.

Overfishing

Unsustainable fishing depletes populations and disrupts food chains.

Ocean Acidification

Climate change causes acidification, killing corals and shellfish.

How You Can Help

  • Reduce Plastic: Use reusable bags and avoid single-use plastics.
  • Support Sustainable Seafood: Choose certified eco-friendly options.
  • Donate: Fund organizations like Ocean Conservancy or Greenpeace.
  • Advocate: Push for marine protected areas and cleaner policies.
