About Wildlife Conservation

Forests are home to millions of species, but they're under threat from human activities. From tigers in the Amazon to elephants in Africa, wildlife faces extinction. Our mission is to raise awareness and drive change.

Major Threats to Wildlife

Deforestation

Logging and agriculture destroy habitats, leading to species loss.

Poaching

Illegal hunting threatens animals like rhinos and pandas.

Climate Change

Rising temperatures disrupt ecosystems and migration patterns.

How You Can Help

  • Donate: Support organizations like WWF or Rainforest Alliance.
  • Reduce Waste: Go eco-friendly to cut pollution.
  • Advocate: Spread awareness on social media.
  • Volunteer: Join local conservation efforts.
