When to Seek Emergency Care?

Difficulty breathing, severe injury, poisoning, seizures, excessive bleeding, or sudden collapse are all signs that your pet needs immediate medical attention.

What You Should Do

Stay calm, keep your pet safe, avoid giving food or medication without advice, and contact a veterinarian immediately using the numbers above.
