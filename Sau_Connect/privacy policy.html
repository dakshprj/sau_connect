Our Commitment to Your Privacy

We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share it only in the following cases:

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data, including encryption and access controls. However, no method is 100% secure.
